Custom-made vibrant, whimsical pet portraits are available. An original Amanda Pampena acrylic painting of your beloved animal companion makes a unique gift and expression of your love for animals. From dogs to cats, tarantulas to lizards...all species and breeds are welcome!

Paintings are made from pictures you provide. It is best to send a high quality photo with good lighting. At least 1200 pixels wide is preferred, though I can try to work with a smaller size. Please include your pet's name and something about his or her personality.

For a quote, please send an email or leave a message at (720) 675-7394.

These are acrylic paintings on stretched canvas with painted sides, complete with hanging wire on the back. Paintings usually take 4-6 weeks to complete. Let me know if there is a particular date by which the painting needs to be finished and plan accordingly. I may be able to rush orders at an additional cost depending on current demand.

A non-refundable deposit of 20% is required before starting the portrait. The final piece will be photographed and emailed to you before shipping. The remaining cost plus shipping is due before shipment. I accept cash, checks (held until cleared), and Paypal.

Contact me with further questions or to commission a pet portrait. I am also available for web design/development.

Amanda Pampena graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Drawing and Painting from the University of Colorado Denver. Her animal companions act as models for her work. These include cats, dogs, and leopard geckos. She also paints other subjects such as stuffed animal still-life and human figures.

Her influences are Egon Schiele, Xu Beihong, and Theophile Steinlen. Pampena studied under professors Mary K. Connelly and Stefan Kleinschuster. She has shown in galleries such as Emmanuel Gallery in Denver, CO and West Nebraska Arts Center in Scottsbluff, NE. She also does web design and development. Pampena currently resides in Sunnyvale, CA.
